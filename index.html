Update

Why Update Your Izzo Golf GPS?

Regularly updating your Izzo Golf GPS device ensures you're playing with the most accurate and current course data. Updated software improves location tracking, battery performance, and adds new features or bug fixes to enhance your game.

Benefits of the Latest GPS Firmware

  • Improved accuracy and tracking
  • Updated course maps and data
  • Faster startup and better battery life
  • New features and performance enhancements

How to Perform the Update

  1. Connect your Izzo Golf GPS to your computer via USB.
  2. Visit the official update portal: Navisolve.com
  3. Download the latest firmware or software update package.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the update.
  5. Once done, safely disconnect and restart your GPS device.

Troubleshooting Tips

If the update fails or your device doesn't respond, ensure the USB cable is secure and your internet connection is stable. Try restarting your device and computer before repeating the process. For persistent issues, contact Izzo Golf support.
